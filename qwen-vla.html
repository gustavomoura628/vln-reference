Qwen-VLA

Qwen3.5-4B · DiT decoder · 2026 · arXiv 2605.30280

Mechanism

Qwen-VLA pairs a Qwen3.5-4B vision-language backbone with a DiT-based action decoder. A single set of weights absorbs supervision from diverse embodied datasets, covering both manipulation and navigation. For navigation it predicts waypoints as (Δx, Δy, Δθ) — the relative displacement and heading change per step. It is trained with a progressive recipe of action pretraining, multimodal continued pretraining, supervised fine-tuning, and reinforcement learning.

Evaluation

On VLN-CE Val-Unseen the paper reports an R2R success rate of 57.5, ahead of the four specialist baselines on success rate. No code or weights are released, so these numbers are not independently reproducible.
